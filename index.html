About Me

I'm a programmer focused on C++ rendering and Lua addon development for World of Warcraft. My two public projects — mQoL and DungeonTeleportsTab — are maintained and updated on a regular basis.

Both addons are open source: each one has a page here with a feature overview, screenshots, and a complete changelog in English and Polish.

What you'll find here

mQoL

A modular quality-of-life addon: account-wide overview of gold, professions, played time and Great Vault progress, UI settings sync across characters, fast autoloot, and Blizzard bug fixes. Over 220 published builds.

DungeonTeleportsTab

A dungeon teleport tab built into the Group Finder: expansion filters, the current season by default, and unlock info for every portal. Available for Midnight and MoP Classic.
